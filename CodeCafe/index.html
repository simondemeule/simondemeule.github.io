Code & Café

Code & Café is Jacques Nacouzi and Christine Durant's labour of love. It is a café at the heart of Plateau Mont-Royal that triples as a café, a workspace, and a space where children, adults and families can come learn programming and robotics.

I have had the pleasure of working with them on preparing content for the opening of their small buisness, and I currently am working as a teacher for them while also continuing to produce content.

My work consists in the production of a series of illustrated robotics workshops (pictured above), and the expansion of the visual brand image created by Intégral Jean Beaudoin into a responsive website and various fliers and posters (a sample of which is pictured below).
