Cohesion

Cohesion is a collection of original musical works assembled over the past few years, which have converged towards a personal aesthetic defined by the borrowing of elements of electronic and classical music â€” the sound and techniques of music concrete, the simplicity of ambient electronic, the aleatoric and generative composition techniques of minimalist classical, the chromaticism, ambiguity and unfamiliar harmonies of impressionist classical. (2018)

All tracks are instrumental, featuring cello and piano performed by myself, along with modular synthesizer, field recordings and various sound objects. Some of the tracks feature Maxime Giard as a co-creator.

Above is my home studio, where most of the material was created.

Music, sound art and music technology have been at the very center of my practice in the last few years, and I seek to pursue this passion in my career.
