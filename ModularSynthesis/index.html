Modular Synthesis

This is an analog synthesizer I hand built in 2016. From bare copper boards, integrated circuits, passive components, wires and acrylic I assembled it over the course of a year, following the open source plans authored by Yves Usson.

After having encountered modularity in shading, colors and textures and color in the 3D software Blender, the idea of applying the same principles to sound seduced me.
