Modular Synth

This is an analog music synthesizer I hand built in 2016. From bare copper boards, integrated circuits, passive components, wires and acrylic I assembled it over the course of a year, following the open source plans authored by Yves Usson.

After having encountered modularity in shading, colors and textures and color in the 3D software Blender, the idea of applying the same principles to sound seduced me.

Since then, I have dived deeper into the mathematics and psychology of sound and music, and have sustained a deep interest in sound art and electroacoustic music.
