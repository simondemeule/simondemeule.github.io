Axis Mundi

Axis Mundi is collaborative performance in which I created animated images that were projected onto dancing sails, giving life and motion to water. Axis Mundi was collectively created by Espace Art Nature over many years, and incorporates elements from legends and spirtuality of the native people. My goal was to create something that faithfully resonated with the group's vision.

My creative process involved 3D modeling, shader design, animation, batch editing and compositing, time-lapse photography, and was held all together by a small program I worte on Processing, which displayed the image sequences I created, cycling continuously and fading in or out on cue.

You can take a look at this program written for Processing 3 on my GitHub here.

Presented at Studios Méduse in october 2016.
