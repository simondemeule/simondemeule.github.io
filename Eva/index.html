Eva

Eva is an abstract short film coupled with a musique concrète inspired sound collage. Both images and sounds were captured at the same location — a very special thrift shop — and together seek to evoke the place's spirit — mystery, strangeness, chaos and beauty. (2018)

The visual artifacts, which enforce the odd character of the movie, were created by abusing an edge-synthesis based video stabilizer. The video editing focused on accentuating the musical gestures through the images' movements.
